SMS platform empowering lifesaving drone deliveries in rural areas.
powered by Tropo and Spark

the zdrone platform

zdrone is a free application enabling rural healthcare providers to order lifesaving healthcare supplies with just a short phone call. currently, there are drone companies like zipline servicing rural areas in Rawanda with blood and other supplies that are hard to transport through road. by building a simple user interface for healthcare providers to communicate to such drone warehouses about what supplies to deliver, the process can be more easily streamlined.
